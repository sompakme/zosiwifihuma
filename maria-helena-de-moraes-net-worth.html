Maria Helena de Moraes is a Brazilian businesswoman who has an estimated net worth of $5.95 billion as of March 2017, according to Bloomberg.

Maria Helena de Moraes inherited control of Votorantim Group from her father, Antônio Ermírio de Moraes, who died in 2014. The company is the largest cement maker in Brazil which has around 38,700 workers across the Americas.

Maria Helena Moraes Scripilliti was born on September 30, 1930. She has four children with his husband, the late Jose Ermirio de Moraes.
