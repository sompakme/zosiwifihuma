Kenny Omega And Chris Jericho To Team Up On 11/1 AEW Dynamite

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Chris Jericho and Kenny Omega will join forces on the November 1 episode of AEW Dynamite.

AEW CEO Tony Khan announced that Chris Jericho and Kenny Omega will face “Daddy Magic” Matt Menard and “Cool Hand Ang” Angelo Parker. Don Callis recruited Menard and Parker to face the former world champions on the October 27 episode of AEW Rampage.

Updated Card For 11/1 AEW Dynamite

The updated card for Wednesday’s show is as follows:

  • : Orange Cassidy (c) vs. Claudio Castagnoli
  • MJF & TBA vs. Bullet Club Gold (Jay White, Juice Robinson & The Gunns)
  • Kenny Omega & Chris Jericho vs. Matt Menard & Angelo Parker

WrestleZone will have coverage of AEW Dynamite as it airs on November 1.
