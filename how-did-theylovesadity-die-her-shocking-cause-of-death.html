How did Theylovesadity die? Her shocking cause of death

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

TikTok fans are mourning the death of one of its beloved creators, Theylovesadity – real name Asia LaFlora. Asia grew her following by posting body-positivity content – she expressed confidence about her body and encouraged her fans to do the same.

For an 18-year-old, LaFlora showed maturity beyond her years. She was a beacon of positivity for half-a-million people, and, considering the rapid growth of her following, she was destined to become one of the platform’s most popular stars. Unfortunately, death took Theylovesadity away before she realized her potential. 

Asia reportedly committed suicide using a firearm

Reports claim that Theylovesadity committed suicide using a firearm. According to Dexerto, Asia’s body was found in California, and authorities don’t suspect foul play – they are investigating the death as a suicide. 

Instagram stories posts by Asia’s sister Yanna (username _poohya) seemingly confirm Asia committed suicide. Yanna wrote: “Heartbroken asf! Please pray for me and my family. God oh idk wtf going on but lord we need you, Asia why! I loved you so much! Why tf did you do this!”

A GoFundMe page has been created to find Asia’s homegoing service. It reads: “It’s with deep sadness and heavy hearts that we have to share the unexpected passing of Asia LaFlora on 1/4/23. Asia was a loving, beautiful, smart, and intelligent person. A life so beautifully lived and deserves to be beautifully remembered.”

Fans have posted numerous online tributes to LaFlora, demonstrating how much her fanbase adored her. @fendibombshells tweeted:

“I can’t believe she’s really gone. I watch her videos all the time and to wake up to this news is crazy. I deal with mental health issues myself and she was one of the influencers I would watch to feel better. It’s so sad that she lost her battle.”
