About Andrew Santino

Andrew Santino is a well-known comedian and actor. He has entertained audiences through his various comedy ventures, including TV shows, podcasts, and stand-up performances. As an actor, Santino has showcased his comedic chops in popular series such as FX’s “Dave” and SHOWTIME’s “I’m Dying Up Here.”

Aside from his acting career, Santino has made a name for himself in the podcasting world. He hosts his own solo podcast called “Whiskey Ginger,” where he engages in humorous and candid conversations with notable guests. Additionally, he co-hosts the widely popular podcast “Bad Friends” alongside comedian Bobby Lee, combining their unique comedic chemistry to entertain listeners.

Santino’s talent isn’t limited to the small screen and podcasting world; he’s also an exceptional stand-up comedian. He has performed at various venues across the country, delivering side-splitting sets that showcase his quick wit and infectious energy. Santino’s comedic prowess extends beyond the stage, as he has been a featured performer at the prestigious Just for Laughs Comedy Festival in Montreal.

With his captivating performances, Santino has garnered a devoted fanbase who appreciate his comedic charm both on and off the stage. Whether he’s making audiences laugh through his acting, podcasting, or stand-up, Andrew Santino continues to captivate audiences with his wit and comedic brilliance.

About Bobby Lee

Bobby Lee is a versatile comedian, known for his impressive body of work in television, stand-up, and podcasting. He gained widespread recognition as a cast member on the popular sketch comedy show MAD TV. Lee’s comedic talent and unique style have also made him a household name in the podcasting world with his highly successful comedy podcast, Tiger Belly.

Aside from his podcast and television career, Bobby Lee has made memorable appearances in various TV shows and movies, showcasing his comedic chops. He has brought his comedic flair to shows like “Splitting Up Together” and “Curb Your Enthusiasm,” leaving audiences in stitches.

Stand-Up Comedy and Unpredictable Performances

Bobby Lee’s stand-up comedy is a must-see experience. With his fearless approach and uncensored humor, he has captivated audiences across the country. His performances are known for their unpredictability and hilarious anecdotes, creating a unique connection with the audience.

Movie Appearances

Bobby Lee’s talent extends to the silver screen as well. He has showcased his acting abilities in hit movies such as “The Dictator” and “Harold & Kumar Go to White Castle,” delivering memorable performances that add to his comedic repertoire.

With his multifaceted talents, Bobby Lee continues to make a significant impact in the comedy industry. Whether through his stand-up comedy, entertaining podcast, or memorable appearances on TV and in movies, his comedic genius shines through, leaving audiences wanting more.
