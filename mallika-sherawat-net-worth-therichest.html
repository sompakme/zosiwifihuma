Mallika Sherawat is a Bollywood film actress who is best known for bold on-screen attitude. As of the moment, her net worth is at $10 million. She is often considered by media as a sex symbol. Before she made into the film industry, Mallika Sherawat started as a commercial model and music video artist. Her impressive portrayal of role in the Murder, a film inspired by Unfaithful in Hollywood, earned her a nomination for best actress.

Her first international career started in 2005 when she played a role in The Myth starring Jackie Chan. She played the role of an Indian women who saved Jackie Chan's character in the river. She then appeared at the Cannes Festival where she was hailed as "the next big thing" by Richard Corliss of TIME magazine. She also appeared in other Hollywood films such as Politics of Love and Hisss.
