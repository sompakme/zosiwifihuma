Becky Lynch Takes More Shots at Ronda Rousey on Twitter

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 10, 2024

The feud between Becky Lynch and Ronda Rousey has gone from a small rivalry to a full-blown hatred for each other, and with their triple threat match at WrestleMania 35 only days away, Lynch has taken to Twitter to once again take shots at Rousey.

In a tweet shared earlier today, Lynch referenced Rousey’s remarks about her mother never teaching her to lose by saying that she would do what her mother didn’t. “you said your mother only showed you how to win, but never how to lose. Well, this Sunday The Man is going to learn you what your mother didn’t teach you.“ said Lynch.

This comes just days after the two were involved in a backstage battle that ended with both of them being tossed into the back of a police car. Needless to say, the upcoming main event for WrestleMania 35 is quickly reaching “must watch” territory as this feud continues to grow.
