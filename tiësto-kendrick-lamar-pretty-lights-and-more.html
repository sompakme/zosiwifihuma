Tisto, Kendrick Lamar, Pretty Lights and more

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

While most ravers are getting their minds and bodies prepared for the shores of South Beach in mid-March, they may forget that Miami is not the only city that can throw down a hot dance party. The heartland’s Kanrocksas Music Festival prepares for a second year by announcing their complete lineup, and its a scorcher! Tiësto, Kendrick Lamar, and Pretty Lights are just a few of the all-star acts who will be gracing the stages at Kansas City‘s Kansas Speedway from June 28 to 29.

Tickets are still available at kanrocksas.com, and be sure to take a look at the complete lineup list below, brought to you first by yours truly:
