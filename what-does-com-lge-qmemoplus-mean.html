What does com lge qmemoplus mean

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 20, 2024

What is COM LGE Qmemoplus?

It’s LG’s QuickMemo+ app. Even if you don’t use an app, it may periodically ping your Google account to refresh data or location. FYI, “lge” means LG Electronics, so you know it’s official.

What is COM LGE email mean?

Folks, “LGE” means LG Electronics. This is LG’s stock email app, and it’s in your activity because your email app is periodically checking for email. Don’t worry about it.

What is Quick Memo Plus on Android?

The QuickMemo+ feature allows you to capture screen shots and use them to create memos.

What does used com LGE camera mean?

It just looks like the name of the pre-installed LG camera app. Does it show up in an activity log or something like that? If so, it probably means that the camera app was opened – but it doesn’t necessarily imply that any photos were taken.
