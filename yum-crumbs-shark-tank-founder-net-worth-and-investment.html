Yum Crumbs Shark Tank Founder, Net Worth, and Investment

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

Yum Crumbs, the innovative dessert topping company, made waves on Episode 07 of Season 15 of Shark Tank. Founded in 2020 by Delson Jeanvilma, Yum Crumbs specializes in a variety of crumb and candy toppings designed to elevate the taste and texture of desserts. With a wide range of flavors and a commitment to quality, Yum Crumbs has quickly become a favorite among bakers and dessert enthusiasts.

During their appearance on Shark Tank, Yum Crumbs sought a $100,000 investment for a 10% equity stake. However, the budding company ended up accepting a deal for $100,000 in exchange for a 20% equity stake with Barbara Corcoran and Daymond John. This partnership has proved to be a game-changer for Yum Crumbs, propelling them towards even greater success in the dessert toppings market.

About Yum Crumbs and their Products

Yum Crumbs is a dessert topping company that offers a wide range of flavors to enhance the taste and texture of desserts. Their versatile products can be mixed into batter or sprinkled on top of various sweet treats. They have over 20 flavors to choose from, allowing customers to create unique and personalized desserts. Yum Crumbs’ products are certified by the FDA, ensuring quality and safety. They are known for saving time for bakers by eliminating the need to make crumbs for crumb cakes.

With their extensive range of flavors, customers can explore new and exciting combinations to create mouth-watering desserts. Their dessert toppings are incredibly versatile, giving bakers and home cooks the freedom to experiment and add an extra burst of flavor to their creations. The toppings can be easily incorporated into batters for cakes, cookies, and muffins, or simply used as a delicious sprinkle on top of ice cream, pies, or fruit salads. The possibilities are endless, and Yum Crumbs’ products allow for endless creative experimentation.

The Journey of Yum Crumbs and its Founder

Yum Crumbs was founded by Delson Jeanvilma and his family in 2020. Delson, along with his son, is an entrepreneur with a passion for innovation in the dessert industry. The idea for Yum Crumbs came from their own experience as bakers, realizing the labor-intensive process of making crumbs for crumb cakes. They conducted research and testing to create a streamlined solution while maintaining quality and taste. The Jeanvilma family, including Delson, his wife Amanda, their five children, and their grandmother, all played a role in establishing the brand. Yum Crumbs is a family-owned business.

Yum Crumbs’ Success and Offer on Shark Tank

Before appearing on Shark Tank, Yum Crumbs had already established itself as a successful dessert toppings brand. They offered a wide range of flavors and had gained popularity among consumers for their delicious and innovative products. On Shark Tank, Yum Crumbs sought a $100,000 investment for a 10% equity stake. They received offers from Barbara Corcoran and Daymond John as well as Kevin O’Leary. Ultimately, they accepted a deal from Barbara Corcoran and Daymond John for $100,000 in exchange for a 20% equity stake. The Sharks were impressed by Yum Crumbs and its potential in the dessert toppings market.
