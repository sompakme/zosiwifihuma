Tara Reid Goes Bowling in Her Bra to Recreate Big Lebowski Character

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 6, 2024

Back to her Bunny suit! Tara Reid continued her wild Instagram spree in the early hours of Wednesday, Jan. 7, shedding her clothes at Lucky Strike in San Francisco to once again become her character from The Big Lebowski. 

The actress, 39, lost her shirt to go bowling in her bra, using the moment to pay homage to Bunny Lebowski from the 1998 cult classic. 

Though Bunny was known for her famous bikini and dirty mouth, Reid made do with a plunging black bra and took a page out of main character The Dude’s book by doing a little bowling. Famously played by Jeff Bridges, The Dude is a big bowler and hangs out with a crew of equally enthusiastic players at his local bowling alley.

“#bunnylebowski just got a strike,” Reid captioned a shot of herself sitting on the lane in the bra and a pair of white jeans with a bowling ball on her lap. 

In the Coen Brothers‘ flick, Bunny is the gold-digging wife of Jeffrey the “Big” Lebowski (David Huddleston) — the man that The Dude gets mistaken for. 

After making an earlier reference to the character with a shot of Bunny’s classic lines from The Big Lebowski, Reid took her character development a step further. She posted a subsequent Instagram photo and posed in her bra on the lane once more, this time straddling two bowling balls. “Having balls of fun!” she wrote.
