Jia Yueting

Birthname Jia Yueting Education University of Shanxi Profession Businessman Net worth $4,800,000,000 Source of Wealth Online Video Nationality Chinese Country China Marital Status Married

Chinese businessman Jia Yueting has an estimated net worth of $4.8 billion as of April 2016, according to Forbes. He currently chairs the Leshi Television which is considered as one of the famous online video websites in China.

Jia Yueting is also known to be the founder of LeTV that strives in providing cross-platform internet video services in the country. He is credited for making a significant change in the field of traditional television when he made contents available online. He also founded the Beijing Siebel Technology, LeTV film corporation, and Shanxi Siebel Technology.

Jia Yueting currently holds an MBA degree from the University of Shanxi.
