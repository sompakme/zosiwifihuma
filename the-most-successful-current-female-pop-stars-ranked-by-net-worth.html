The Most Successful Current Female Pop Stars Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 8, 2024

While the past year has certainly been quite successful for plenty of female pop stars — today’s list takes a look at those who have been in the spotlight the most. Some released albums in 2020, others collaborate with other famous artists, and a few even released their very own documentaries (yes — we're talking about Miss Americana and Ariana Grande: Excuse Me, I Love You).

Related: 10 Most Successful The Voice Coaches, Ranked By Net Worth

From former Disney Channel stars such as Miley Cyrus and Selena Gomez to musicians that are still quite new to the industry like Dua Lipa and Billie Eilish — keep on scrolling to find out which female pop stars are currently the richest!

10 Camila Cabello — Net Worth $14 Million

Kicking the list off at spot number 10 is former Fifth Harmony member Camila Cabello who certainly achieved quite a lot since leaving the girl band in 2016. While Camila hasn’t released much new music in 2020 — besides the Holiday song "The Christmas Song" with Shawn Mendes — her 2019 album Romance was still quite successful. Last year Camila was supposed to kick off her Romance Tour, unfortunately, it ended up getting canceled because of the ongoing coronavirus pandemic. Currently, the star is estimated to have a net worth of $14 million.

9 Halsey — Net Worth $16 Million

Let’s move on to singer Halsey who certainly had a very successful year behind her. On January 17, 2020, Halsey released her third studio album called Manic from which the singles "Without Me", "Graveyard", and "You Should Be Sad" became big hits. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Halsey — who is known for her colorful outfits and even crazier hairstyles — is currently estimated to have a net worth of $16 million.

8 Dua Lipa — Net Worth $16 Million

Spot number eight on today’s list goes to British singer Dua Lipa who is definitely one of the few celebs on today’s list who had an incredibly successful year behind her. On March 27, 2020, Dua released her second studio album Future Nostalgia which quickly became a fan favorite with its such as "Physical" and "Break My Heart".

In 2020, Dua also collaborated with fellow artist Miley Cyrus on her song "Prisoner" which immediately became a hit as well. Currently, Dua — known for her stylish stage looks — is estimated to have a net worth of $16 million which means she stares her spot with Halsey.

7 Billie Eilish — Net Worth $25 Million

Musician Billie Eilish is also on today’s list as the singer currently has an estimated net worth of $25 million. While Billy released her super successful debut studio album When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? in 2019 — last year she released a couple of singles such as "No Time to Die" (the theme song for the upcoming James Bond movie), as well as "My Future" and "Therefore I Am". Needless to say, everything that Billie releases quickly becomes very popular!

6 Lana Del Rey — Net Worth $30 Million

Singer Lana Del Rey also found herself on today’s list as the star released and was featured on a couple of singles in the past year such as "Let Me Love You Like a Woman" and "Hallucinogenics". Lana's sixth studio album was released in 2019 and her seventh one titled Chemtrails over the Country Club is expected to be released this year. Currently, Lana Del Rey is estimated to have a net worth of $30 million.

5 Selena Gomez — Net Worth $75 Million

Opening up the top five most successful female musicians ranked by their current net worth is former Disney Channel star, Selena Gomez. On January 10, 2020, Selena released her third solo studio album Rare which included hits such as "Lose You to Love Me" and "Look at Her Now". According to Celebrity Net Worth, Selena is currently estimated to have a net worth of $75 million.

4 Ariana Grande — Net Worth $150 Million

Spot number four on today’s list goes to pop diva Ariana Grande who released her sixth studio album Positions on October 30, 2020.

From the album, which features guest appearances from Doja Cat, The Weeknd, and Ty Dolla $ign, two singles have already been released — "Positions" and "34+35". Currently, Ariana Grande is estimated to have a net worth of $150 million.

3 Miley Cyrus — Net Worth $160 Million

Next on the list is Miley Cyrus who has certainly had a very impressive career since her Disney Channel days. In 2020, Miley released her very anticipated seventh studio album Plastic Hearts with which she certainly explored different sounds. So far, Miley released two singles from it — "Midnight Sky" and "Prisoner", and both were major successes. Currently, the former Disney Channel star is estimated to have a net worth of $160 million.

2 Katy Perry — Net Worth $330 Million

The runner-up on today’s list is popstar Katy Perry with an estimated net worth of $330 million. 2020 was a significant year in Katy Perry's life both — professionally and privately, as the star not only released her sixth studio album Smile but she also welcomed her first child — daughter Daisy Dove Bloom into the world.

1 Taylor Swift — Net Worth $400 Million

Wrapping the list up at spot number one with a whopping net worth of $400 million is Taylor Swift. The musician not only released one album in 2020 but she actually released two — and both were major successes. 2020 started out with Taylor releasing her documentary Miss Americana on Netflix and it ended with the release of her eighth studio album Folklore and her ninth studio album Evermore!
