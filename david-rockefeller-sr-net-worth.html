David Rockefeller Sr. Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 9, 2024
David Rockefeller Sr.

Birthname David Rockefeller Sr. Birthdate June 12, 1915 Birthplace New York City, New York, U.S. Education Doctorate, University of Chicago; Bachelor of Arts / Science, Harvard University Profession Businessman Net worth $3,000,000,000 Source of Wealth Oil, Banking Nationality United States Country United States Marital Status Married (Peggy McGrath) Children 5 (avid Jr., Abby, Peggy, Eileen, Richard)

One of well known surviving members of the famous Rockefeller family, David Rockefeller is an American philanthropist and banker with an estimated net worth of $3 billion as of April 2016 according to Forbes. With his $3 billion net worth, he was named by Forbes as the 49th richest person in the world.

Born to Abby Aldrich Rockefeller and John D. Rockefeller, Jr., David Rockefeller’s other siblings were Nelson, Laurance, Winthrop, John D. III and Abby. He is also the only surviving grandchild of John D. Rockefeller, a well-known oil tycoon who founded the Standard Oil.

After graduating from Harvard University as Cum Laude, Rockefeller went on to study at London School of Economics, then to the University of Chicago where he finished his Ph.D. in Economics.

In 1946, he officially joined Chase National Bank which was later known as Chase Manhattan Bank. Through time, the institution significantly changed into consumer banking and today, it is known as JP Morgan Chase.

Starting as an assistant manager at the Foreign Department, David Rockefeller worked his way up the ladder until he became the company’s president in 1960. From 1961 through 1980, he served as the bank’s Chief executive and Chairman of the Board.

David Rockefeller was married to Margaret “Peggy” McGrath until the latter’s death in 1996. Their 55-year marriage resulted to the birth of their six children named David Jr. (b. 1941), Abby (b. 1943), Neva (b. 1944), Peggy (b. 1947), Richard (b. 1949), and Eileen (b. 1952).
