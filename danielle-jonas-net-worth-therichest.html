American reality television personality and former hairdresser, Danielle Jonas has an estimated net worth $3 million.

Jonas was born on January 1, 1990. In 2007, she met Kevin Jonas, who is one-third of the popular music trio "The Jonas Brothers." After two years of dating, Kevin proposed to her in July 2009. On December 19 of the same year, the couple got married. Currently, Danielle and Kevin Jonas are seen on Married to Jonas, a reality TV show which chronicles their lives as a couple. The couple is currently based in Denville, New Jersey, USA.

She lived a fairly normal life until she met Kevin Jonas, the oldest member of the immensely popular trio, The Jonas Brothers. The two met while both were vacationing in The Bahamas in 2007. The relationship was kept secret from the press and public for almost a year until photos were published of the pair kissing in late 2008. In 2009, after Mr. Jonas flew from Canada to New Jersey to ask for her hand, the two were married at Oheka Castle, West Hills, New York, the United States in the winter of the same year. Beginning in the fall of 2012, they will appear on the reality series, “Married to Jonas”. The show chronicles their lives together, and the release of the Jonas Brothers’ next album.
