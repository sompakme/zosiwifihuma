Rihanna &amp; Coldplay Headline Made in America 2016

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 3, 2024

After five years in the mix, Budweiser and Jay Z’s Made in America festival just keeps getting better.

Rihanna and Coldplay have been announced as headliners for the 2016 M.I.A festival, which is set to take place at Philadelphia’s Benjamin Franklin Parkway over Labor Day Weekend.

Although it doesn’t look like Budweiser and Hov will return to the bi-costal format from a couple years ago, they make up for it with plenty of talent on the bill.

Besides Rih Rih and Coldplay, performers include Chance The Rapper, Bryson Tiller, Lil Wayne, Desiigner, Lil Uzi Vert, 2 Chainz, A$AP Ferg, SZA, and more.

Pre-sale tickets will be available through TIDAL on Wednesday (June 22) at 10 a.m. EST, while general admission passes go on sale Monday, June 27.

The festival goes down Saturday, Sept. 3  through Sunday, Sept. 4.
