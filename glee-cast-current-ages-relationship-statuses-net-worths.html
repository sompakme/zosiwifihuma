Glee Cast: Current Ages, Relationship Statuses, &amp; Net Worths

The cast of Glee parted ways in 2015 after six amazing seasons of the show. After the first episode aired in 2009, everyone knew that it was going to be a hit since the show included so many amazing cover songs of amazing bands and musicians. Some of the songs that were covered on Glee included music By Adele, Aerosmith, Alanis Morissette, and even Alicia Keys!

Unfortunately, Naya Rivera's life ended in June 2020 after a boating accident occurred. No one knows for certain about the details of her death except for the fact that she went out on a boat with her toddler-aged child and did not return. They recovered her body from the bottom of the lake after several days of searching. She passed away at the age of 33 with a $5 million net worth.
