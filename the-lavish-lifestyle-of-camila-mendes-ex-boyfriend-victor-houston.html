The Lavish Lifestyle Of Camila Mendes Ex-Boyfriend Victor Houston

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 8, 2024

Highlights

  • Victor Houston, Camila Mendes' ex-boyfriend, is involved with two bars in NYC.
  • Mendes and Houston ended their relationship in 2018.
  • Mendes is currently in a relationship with Rudy Mancuso.

After starring on the juicy CW series for 7 seasons, the Riverdale actors have been honest about the show. That includes Camila Mendes, who became well-known thanks to her role as feisty and sassy Veronica Lodge. Fans can always count on Mendes to explain what she's actually thinking about, especially when she's talking about her eating disorder and explaining how her body image journey affected her acting career.

Camila Mendes isn't quite as chatty about her love life, but she has had a few interesting relationships. While she and Victor Houston are no longer together, he lives a fascinating lifestyle that her fans will definitely want to hear about.

What Is Camila Mendes' Ex Victor Houston Doing Now?

Camila Mendes' ex-boyfriend Victor Houston is involved with two bars in New York City. His Instagram bio mentions two places: Micro Heaven and Lassiez Faire. Micro Haven is described as a "listening bar" and "microclub" on Instagram, and Laissez Faire opened in February 2023. In an Instagram post from August 2023, Houston called Laissez Faire "a contemporary iteration of the hotel club/lounge."

From looking at Victor Houston's Instagram account, it seems clear that he lives a great lifestyle and has a lot of fun. While his net worth is unknown, he seems to be more than comfortable, and this enables him to travel where he wants.

Houston's life includes a lot of travel and getting together with friends. He must be earning a great salary that allows him to travel whenever he wants to as this seems to be a huge part of his routine. In 2019, he posted photos from London and Paris, and in April 2021, he traveled to St Maarten.

In July 2023, Houston shared photos of a beach trip that he took with a large group of friends. In June 2022, he posted photos of himself on several Greek beaches, which looked like an incredible vacation. He wrote in the Instagram caption:

"Guys! I found these hidden gem islands in Greece! Must visit before they go mainstream and become too touristy."

Victor Houston not only travels internationally, which can add up quickly, but he travels to many fancy places within the U.S. as well. His Instagram includes photos of a trip to Montauk, The Hamptons, New York.

While it's unclear if he has a house in the Hamptons, he does seem to spend a lot of time in Bridgehampton, New York. Every summer, he posts several photos of himself enjoying some time by the pool.

Houston also attended a wedding at The Breakers Palm Beach in June 2021. The hotel is located in Palm Beach, Florida, and is very expensive. Even just one night would break the bank for most people, and since he was there for a wedding, the guests might have spent quite a few nights there.

Houston also seems to go skiing in Aspen fairly often. Aspen is known as a pricey destination so those trips must cost quite a lot.

Victor Houston and Camila Mendes dated briefly several years ago, and he seems to be in a new relationship. He posts many photos of his girlfriend on his Instagram. In September 2021, he wrote in a caption, "Happy birthday to this beautiful lady" and tagged Alexa Shteren. Very little information is available about her, but they seem to be going strong. Shteren posted several recent photos of her and Houston together.

What Happened Between Camila Mendes And Victor Houston?

In July 2018, E! News reported that Victor Houston and Camila Mendes were in a relationship. The publication said that when they were teenagers living in Florida, they went to schools that were close together.

While not a lot is known about their relationship, it seems that this is how they met. People called them "childhood friends." Both E! News and People shared that there were a few photos of the couple on Instagram and that they had been together for two months.

In August 2018, E! News reported that Victor Houston and Camila Mendes ended their romance.

A source explained to the publication:

"Camila ended things with Victor after ComicCon, when she realized she wasn't ready to be in this relationship anymore. Victor was completely blindsided and is very heartbroken. He has been receiving a lot of backlash from the Riverdale community, even though it was actually Camila's choice to end the relationship."

Mendes doesn't seem to have addressed the breakup publicly, but she doesn't say that much about her love life in general. Just like Victor Houston has moved on, Mendes is currently in a relationship. According to Us Weekly, Rudy Mancuso and Camila Mendes were still dating as of September 2023.

While it doesn't seem like Mendes talked about her breakup, she did share a little bit about Houston in 2018. Several sources reported that she chatted about him in an interview with Nylon. The actress said that she was in a relationship with someone who wasn't part of Hollywood:

"It's somebody completely out of the industry. It's funny because I'm more hesitant to talk about it because I don't want him to read this... I actually would totally talk about it right now, if I didn't feel like, Wait, he might read this."
