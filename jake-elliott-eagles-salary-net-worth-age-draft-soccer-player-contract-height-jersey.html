Jake Daniel Elliott is an American football placekicker for the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Memphis. He was part of the Eagles’ Super Bowl LII championship team against the New England Patriots.

Jake Elliott Salary
Elliott earns a whopping sum of $990,000.

Jake Elliott Net Worth

Jake Elliott Age
Elliott was born on January 21, 1995. He is 27-years-old.

Jake Elliott Draft
The Cincinnati Bengals selected Elliott in the fifth round (153rd overall) of the 2017 NFL Draft. He was the first of three kickers selected in 2017.

Jake Elliott Soccer Player
The NFL recently announced that Elliott has been named to the Pro Bowl for the first time.
Elliott is the fourth Eagle to earn Pro Bowl honors this season.

Jake Elliott Contract
On November 27, 2019, Elliot signed a five-year, $21.8 million contract extension with the Eagles through the 2024 season, making him the third-highest paid kicker in the league.
