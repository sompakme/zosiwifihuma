Vinessa Shaw is an American actress, as well as a model who started her acting career at an early age. 

She had her break-out role with Disney’s 1993 Halloween comedy-fantasy film Hocus Pocus.

Husband, Married, Affair

She is married to her long-term boyfriend, Kristopher Gifford, who is a graphic designer. The couple dated for ten years before exchanging the wedding vows in 2017.

Net Worth

Her estimated net worth is $500 thousand, which she has accumulated through her career in acting and modeling.

She started her acting career in 1981 playing the role of Angel Bradley on the flick Home Sweet Home. 

She then appeared in movies like Hocus Pocus, L.A. Without a Map, Eyes Wide Shut, Wayward Son, 40 Days and 40 Nights, Melinda and Melinda, The Hills Have Eyes and more.
