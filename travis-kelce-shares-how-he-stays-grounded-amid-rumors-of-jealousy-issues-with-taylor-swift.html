Travis Kelce Shares How He Stays Grounded Amid Rumors Of Jealousy Issues With Taylor Swift

Highlights

While he didn’t directly address accusations that he’s jealous, Travis Kelce insisted he’s a grounded guy when being grilled about his relationship with Taylor Swift .

During his appearance on Good Morning America on Thursday, Kelce discussed how he maintains his authenticity amidst his high-profile relationship with Taylor Swift. "When you have good family and friends around you, it makes that aspect of life that much easier," he reflected. "I’ve always been a very grounded guy."

Travis Is Allegedly Jealous Of Taylor’s Background Dancers
