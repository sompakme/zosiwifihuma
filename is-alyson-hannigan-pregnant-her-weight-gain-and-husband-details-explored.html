Alyson Hannigan is well known American actress and a Television presenter.

Alyson is known best for her role in How I Met Your Mother, American Pie, and Buffy The Vampire.

Similarly, she works as a television presenter in the show Penn & Teller: Fool Us, and she is also the voice actor in the Disney Junior animation series Fancy Nancy.

Is Alyson Hannigan Pregnant?

No, Alyson Hannigan is not pregnant. Neither Alyson has confirmed the news of her pregnancy on her Twitter account.

The rumours, however, seem to be trending recently without any credible evidence.

Alyson was married to her husband Alexis Denisof in 2003, and the couple is already the parents of two daughters.

Her firstborn, Satyana Marie, was born in March 2009, and her second-born, Keeva Jane, was conceived in May 2012.

Hannigan was pregnant during the How I Met Your Mother with Satyana, but the producers decided to hide her swollen baby hilariously throughout her pregnancy.

Learn About Alyson Hannigan Weight Gain

We don’t think Alyson Hannigan has gain weight so much that she is unrecognizable. She is reaching her 50’s and still seems beautiful than before.

However, back in 2012, people started to confused her weight gain with pregnancy.

Alyson hilariously replies to those rumours, saying it is just too much carnival food, not a baby.

It seems media and people give too much attention to her belly.

Currently, she is working as a TV presenter in Penn & Teller: Fool Us and does voice acting on the Disney Junior animation series Fancy Nancy.

Alyson Hannigan Husband

Alyson Hannigan’s husband is Alexis Denisof, who is also an actor. Alyson and Alexis married in 2003 and are parents to two daughters, Satyana and Keeva.

Alexis is 55 years old, born on February 25, 1966, and is best known for his role in Buffy The Vampire Slayer and its spin-off Angel.

He met his now-wife in Buffy the Vampire Slayer, and still, their relationship is going substantial than ever before. Similarly, he also starred in How I Met Your Mother in a recurring role.

Find him on his Instagram account by the name @alexisdensof, which has more than 65.8k followers.

He shares many photos of his adorable family on his Instagram.
