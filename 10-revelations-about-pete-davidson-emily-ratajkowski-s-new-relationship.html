10 Revelations About Pete Davidson &amp; Emily Ratajkowski's New Relationship

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 30, 2024

Pete Davidson and Emily Ratajkowski are the latest celebrity couple fans didn’t see coming. Both just got out of serious relationships, but it appears they’ve found a rebound in one another. Pete and Emily are both known for keeping their personal lives under wraps, for the most part, but they haven’t been afraid to put their PDA on full display. Here’s everything we know about their relationship so far.RELATED: 8 Women Aaron Carter Dated Over The Years

10 Emily Just Divorced Her Cheating Husband

It was reported in September 2022 that Emily’s divorce from Sebastian Bear-McClard was finalized after reports that he cheated on her.

The couple originally wed in February 2018 after only a few weeks of dating, before welcoming a son, Sylvester, in 2021. However, reports say the exes are now only speaking through their nanny.

9 Emily May Have Left Brad Pitt To Date Pete

Pete isn’t the only guy Emily has been linked to in recent months. Right before she stepped out with Pete, Emily was reportedly dating Brad Pitt.

However, the romance was short-lived, as reports soon confirmed Emily had broken up with the actor, much to Brad’s surprise. It wasn’t long before she was spotted spending time with Pete.

8 Pete Is Traumatized After Dating Kim Kardashian

Though an unlikely couple, Pete began dating Kim Kardashian in the fall of 2021 after meeting while filming a Saturday Night Live episode. The couple appeared head-over-heels. Pete got several tattoos dedicated to Kim and her kids, and he even appeared on The Kardashians.

However, the couple were plagued by harassment from Kim’s ex-husband Kanye West. Pete became the subject of many of his online tirades. Ye encouraged his fans to scream at Pete if they saw him in public, and even released an animated music video of himself burying a likeness of Pete alive.

Pete and Kim eventually split in August 2022, amid reports of conflicting schedules. But Pete also reportedly entered trauma therapy due to Kanye’s harassment.

Emily is the first woman he’s been linked to following his rollercoaster relationship with Kim.

7 They’re Dating, But Likely Not Exclusive

There aren’t a lot of details known about Pete and Emily’s relationship. Both celebrities are known to keep their personal life under wraps, but sources have confirmed they’re dating. But it seems more casual than anything, and maybe not exclusive. “[They are] seeing each other," one source told People.

6 They Haven’t Been Shy To Show PDA

One thing that’s for sure is that Pete and Emily aren’t just friends. They may not have commented on their relationship status yet, but they’re not shy to show PDA in public. The paparazzi have photographed them on numerous occasions holding hands and locking lips, proving that these two are definitely into each other.

5 They’ve Already Been Dating For A Few Months

Pete and Emily were first linked in November 2022, but they may have been seeing each other for a lot longer. Speaking to Us Weekly the same month, a source said the pair have been talking for “a couple of months now.”

“[They’re] in the very early stages, but both really like each other,” they continued.

4 Emily Admitted To Liking Pete Years Ago

During a November 2021 appearance on Late Night Wight Seth Meyers, Emily was asked what about Pete made him so appealing. Though he was dating Kim Kardashian at the time, Emily didn’t hold back revealing how great she thought he was.

“He seems super charming, he’s vulnerable, he’s lovely, his fingernail polish is awesome,” Emily said. “Like, he looks good, he’s great.”

3 She’s Referenced Their Relationship On Social Media

Neither Emily nor Pete have directly commented on their romance, but the model seemingly confirmed they’re seeing each other on social media. Eagle-eyed fans noticed she like a tweet by Dionne Warwick that said, “I will be dating Pete Davidson next.”

2 Pete Likely Hasn’t Met Emily’s Son

There’s no reason to believe that Pete has met Emily’s son with her ex-husband, as the trio hasn’t been photographed together. It’s unclear of the custody arrangement between Emily and her ex.

But Pete has talked about wanting to have kids himself and was spotted hanging out one-on-one with Kim’s kids during their relationship. So, the fact that the model has a child likely is no problem for the comedian. It’s likely just a matter of time before he’s introduced to Emily’s son.

1 Pete Feels Challenged By Emily

The relationship may be easy-going right now, but Pete apparently feels challenged by his new girlfriend, sources say. “Pete and Emily just dig each other and find the vibe they have very easy and free-flowing,” an insider told Us Weekly. “There’s no intensity or over-expectation on either side, just a lot of fun and a chance to cut loose and see where it leads.”

They added, “There’s no doubt Pete loves a challenge and he’s certainly met his intellectual match in Emily.”
