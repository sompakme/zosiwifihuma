A Canadian professional ice hockey goalie who now plays for the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League, Jonathan Bernier was born on August 7, 1988, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada (NHL). He was selected by the Los Angeles Kings in the first round (11th overall) in the 2006 NHL Entry Draft, and he spent his first four seasons in the NHL with the club that selected him. On June 23, 2013, he was dealt to the Toronto Maple Leafs in exchange for Ben Scrivens, Matt Frattin, and a second-round draught selection in the NHL.
