Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 29, 2024
Thomas Lee

Birthname Thomas H. Lee Birthdate March 26, 1944 Birthplace United States Education Harvard College Profession Businessman Net worth $2,000,000,000 Source of Wealth Leveraged Buy-outs Nationality United States Country United States Marital Status Married Children 5

American businessman, investor and financier Thomas H. Lee has an estimated net worth of $2 billion as of April 2016 according to Forbes. Lee is considered to be one of the pioneers of leverage buy-outs and private equity.

After graduating from Harvard College, he started working at L.F. Rothschild as an analyst. Starting in 1966, he worked for First National Bank of Boston for 8 long years until he served the position as Vice President.

In 1974, he founded Thomas H. Lee Partners (THL), which is considered as one of the largest and oldest private equity firms in the world today. He currently serves as Lee Equity Partners' managing partner. Lee Equity Partners is a private equity firm which he founded in 2006.

During its early years, THL purchased Sterling Jewelers for only $28 million. Two years after, Lee sold Sterling Jewelers for $210 million, earning him a profit amounting to $180 million. In 1992, the firm acquired Snapple Beverages and after two years, he sold it to Quaker Oats for a staggering amount of $1.7 billion. Reports claimed that Lee and his investors earned around $900 million for that particular deal.

In 2006, he left Thomas H. Lee Partners (THL) to focus on his newly founded private equity firm, Lee Equity Partners. Thomas Haskell Lee was born on March 26, 1944. He is married and has five children.
