Kylie Jenner Curses Out Guy Who Asks 'What That Mouth Do?'

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

After a considerable number of years being recognized as Kim, Kourtney, Khloe and Kendall’s little sister, Kylie Jenner has morphed into a true member of the Kardashian squad. Garnering her own level of notoriety courtesy of her relationship with Tyga and her temporary lip fillers, the youngest of the reality star bunch can now add cursing a rude photographer out to her rites of celebrity passage.

While out shopping in L.A., Jenner was approached by a man who had no regard for her personal space and was asked quite the disrespectful question: “What that mouth do?” Placing his camera in the young television personality’s face, the guy also referred to her as the wrong sister, Kendall. These count as three offenses all on their own.

In a snarky response to the rude man, Jenner calmy requested her space, specifically by instructing him, “Get the f**k outta my face.” Catch the clip.
