Dinu Patriciu Net Worth | TheRichest

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 31, 2024
Dinu Patriciu

Birthname Dan Costache Patriciu Birthplace Romania Education Bucharest Institute of Architecture Profession Businessman Net worth $1,500,000,000 Source of Wealth Oil, Real Estate, Retail Nationality Romania Marital Status Married Children 2

Once considered as the wealthiest person in Romania, Dinu Patriciu is an entrepreneur, architect and businessman with an estimated net worth of $1.5 billion as of March 2013, according to Forbes.

It was in 1998 when Dinu Patricio led the purchase of the Rompetrol, a Romanian oil company. In 2007, he sold Rompetrol, which was then worth $8.6 billion, to Kazmunaigaz, a state-owned energy operator in Kazakhstan. Currently, Dinu Patricio owns the Adevarul Holding media trust.

In addition to Rompetrol, Dinu Patriciu also earns a great deal of profit from other industries like oil and gas restoration, retail and real estate. Despite his faltering efforts on retail, Dinu Patriciu is still committed to pursue his energy project where he was reported to spend as much as $1 billion to search for gas and black sea oil.
