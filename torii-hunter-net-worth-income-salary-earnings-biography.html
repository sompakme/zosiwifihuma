Torii Hunter Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Torii Kedar Hunter was born on July 18, 1975, in Pine Bluff, Arkansas, in the United States of America. He was a former professional baseball player, most remembered for his time spent with clubs such as the Detroit Tigers, Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim, and Minnesota Twins in Major League Baseball (MLB). He has been named to the All-Star team five times, and his accomplishments have contributed to his current net worth.

What is Torii Hunter’s net worth? In mid-2016, reports estimated that he had a net worth in excess of $60 million, the majority of which was acquired via a successful professional baseball career. Prior to his retirement, he was earning about $10.5 million per year as a professional athlete. He earned nine straight Gold Glove Awards as well as two Silver Slugger Awards throughout his professional baseball career. All of these factors contributed to his wealth’s status.
