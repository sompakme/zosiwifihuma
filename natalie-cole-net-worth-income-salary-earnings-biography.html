Natalie Maria Cole was born on February 6, 1950, in Los Angeles, California, USA, to parents who were also musicians. During the period between the mid-1970s and the early 1990s, she was a very successful singer-songwriter and actor. Songs like “This Will Be,” “Our Love,” and “Inseparable” were written by her and became big singles. She also produced a slew of successful albums and received many accolades, all of which contributed to her impressive financial success and subsequent increase in net worth.

What was Natalie Cole’s net worth? Several sources have said that she had a net worth of more than $5 million at the time of her death, with the majority of the money she accumulated being ascribed to her successful singing career.
