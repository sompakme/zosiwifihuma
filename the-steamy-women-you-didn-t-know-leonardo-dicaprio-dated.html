The Steamy Women You Didn't Know Leonardo DiCaprio Dated

Leonardo DiCaprio, who is a long-time confirmed bachelor, dated some of the most beautiful women on the planet. While he may not be the marrying type, he certainly knows how to be in good company.

His tastes run towards the supermodels that make most men melt by just the sight of them. DiCaprio not only gets to go out with them, he sometimes takes them home, and on vacations with him.

Popsugar made a nice list of many of the women that DiCaprio dated and we chose five of our favorite ones.

Naomi Campbell

Naomi Campbell and Leo DiCaprio have been close friends since 1995. They go on vacations together with a group of mutual friends and help each other with charitable fundraising efforts for the various causes that they support.

Naomi Campbell is originally from England. She started modeling, during the 1980’s, when she was only 15 years old. Soon thereafter, she became one of the most recognizable supermodels in the world. She is one of the top five supermodels from the 1990’s along with Cindy Crawford, Linda Evangelista, Tatjana Patitz, and Christy Turlington.

She broke cultural stereotypes by proving that black models can be as popular and successful on the covers of fashion magazines as any others. She has appeared on television with her reality show called The Face and also had some roles in movies. She is now 47 years old and looks as good as ever.

Helena Christensen

DiCaprio met Helena Christensen during the aftermath of his surging global popularity from his appearance in the film Titanic released in 1997. Young women all over the world swooned for DiCaprio marvelous performance as the romantic lead in that film. Christensen, who is originally from Denmark, was a model for Victoria’s Secret where she became one of the “angels” for that brand.

In 1991, Christensen starred in the music video for Chris Isaak’s song entitled Wicked Game. It was nominated by VH-1 as the fourth sexiest video of all time. During 1996, she joined the list of top supermodels that expanded from five to become “The Magnificent Seven” as declared by the fashion writer Frank DeCaro from the New York Times.

After her flirtations with DiCaprio, she went on to partner up with Norman Reedus, who is the star of The Walking Dead series.

Gisele Bündchen

Gisele Bündchen comes from Brazil. Since 2004, she has been one of the highest paid supermodels in the world. She ranks as one of the wealthiest women in the entertainment/fashion industry. Forbes estimates that during 2016 she made over $30 million that year. She was ranked by Forbes in the top 100 most powerful women in the world.

She made guest appearances in the popular televisions series called Taxi. She also had a supporting role in the movie The Devil Wears Prada that starred Meryl Streep.

After dating DiCaprio for a while, she went on to marry the quarterback from the New England Patriots, Tom Brady, in 2009. By 2011, Forbes named them the highest paid celebrity couple beating the combined earnings of Angelina Jolie with Brad Pitt or Beyoncé with Jay-Z. However, Beyoncé and Jay-Z have since surpassed them.

Kelly Rohrbach

This blonde beauty with stunning green eyes is only 27 years old compared to the 43 years of DiCaprio. DiCaprio started dating Kelly Rohrbach during 2015 and was frequently seen bicycling with her in her hometown of New York City. Rohrback was a Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue model. The couple dated for about a year and went on vacations together to the Hamptons in upstate New York and the exotic location of St.-Tropez. They enjoyed attending the U.S. Open Tennis Tournament where they openly displayed affection for each other.

Rumors were that DiCaprio asked her to marry him; however, this did not turn out to be true or perhaps she said no. After the couple split up, Rohrback went on the get a role in the Baywatch movie with the stars Zac Efron and Dwayne Johnson.

Nina Agdal

Nina Agdal is a 25-year-old model originally from Denmark who now lives in Miami, Florida. In 2013, she was in the Carl’s Jr. Super Bowl commercial. During 2014, she appeared on the cover of the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue. She also appeared on the covers of Maxim and Vogue magazines.

She dated Max George, an English singer from 2013 to 2104. She met DiCaprio during 2016 and went on vacations with him to Montauk, New York, and the Bahamas. The relationship lasted about a year, so once again Leo DiCaprio is still Hollywood’s most eligible bachelor.DiCaprio says he will get married “when it just happens.” In the meantime, he has no lack of steamy beauties to hang out with.
